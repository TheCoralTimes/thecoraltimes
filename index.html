Welcome Falcons!

Lemon Doroshow - March 3rd, 2023

Hello Falcons! Welcome to the Coral Times! We’re a newspaper by the students, for the students. If you’d like to submit articles, give us any recommendations, or even join in on the club, feel free to contact us under the above “Contact Us” tab!
 

In the meantime, make sure to stay updated by checking our site often, joining our Discord server, or following our Instagram! We have lots of exciting content planned for our readers. We look forward to hearing from you guys!
